# Ad Hook Swipe File

Starter collection of proven ad hooks organized by angle. **This file grows automatically** -- when `/ad-analyzer` processes Long-Runner ads (60d+), it appends their hooks here with attribution.

The primary swipe file lives in your Airtable Ad Swipe File table. This markdown file is a quick reference for scripting sessions.

**How it grows:** Add aspirational competitors (Hormozi, Brunson, etc.) to your Competitors table as "Aspirational" tier. They test 100-200 ads at a time. The hooks that survive 60+ days are battle-tested winners. The poller scrapes them, the analyzer extracts hooks from Long-Runners, and they land here automatically. Millions of dollars of A/B testing, harvested for free.

**Format for auto-added hooks:**
```
- "{hook text}" -- [Competitor Name, 90d, Video]
```

---

## Social Proof Hooks

- "[Number] [audience] signed up in the last [timeframe]"
- "Here is why [number] [audience] switched to [solution]"
- "[Name] went from [before] to [after] in [timeframe]"
- "We asked [number] [audience] what changed everything. They all said the same thing."
- "This is the exact [thing] that got [name] [result]"
- "I was not going to share this but [number] people asked"

## Pain-to-Transformation Hooks

- "If you are still [painful thing], you need to see this"
- "Stop [common mistake] -- it is costing you [specific cost]"
- "I used to [painful before state]. Then I [discovered solution]."
- "The reason you are not getting [result] has nothing to do with [common belief]"
- "[Painful thing] is not your fault. But fixing it is your responsibility."
- "Every [audience] I talk to makes the same mistake with [topic]"

## Tips / Education Hooks

- "[Number] things I wish I knew before [starting thing]"
- "The [one/single] [thing] that changed my [result] overnight"
- "Do this before you [common action] -- it will save you [cost]"
- "Most [audience] skip this step. Do not."
- "Here is the [topic] strategy nobody is talking about"
- "I spent [money/time] figuring this out so you do not have to"

## Growth Problem Hooks

- "Your [business/offer] is not growing because of [unexpected reason]"
- "You do not have a [traffic/leads/sales] problem. You have a [real problem]."
- "If [metric] is flat, check this first"
- "[Number] out of [number] [audience] are doing [thing] wrong"
- "The market shifted [timeframe] ago. Most [audience] have not caught up."

## Profit Problem Hooks

- "You are leaving [money amount] on the table every [timeframe]"
- "This one change added [amount] to our [metric] in [timeframe]"
- "The math does not lie -- here is why [common approach] loses money"
- "We cut [cost] by [percentage] and got better results. Here is how."
- "If your [metric] is below [number], you are bleeding money"

## Authority Hooks

- "After [number] [years/clients/campaigns], here is what actually works"
- "I have [managed/spent/generated] [impressive number] in [domain]. This is what I learned."
- "Everyone is telling you to [common advice]. They are wrong."
- "[Industry expert] taught me this and it changed everything"
- "Here is what [impressive result] actually looks like behind the scenes"

## Scarcity / Urgency Hooks

- "We only take [number] new [clients/members] per [timeframe]"
- "This offer closes [date/timeframe] -- here is why"
- "Last chance to [get result] before [event/deadline]"
- "[Number] spots left for [timeframe]"

## Behind-the-Scenes Hooks

- "Here is exactly how we [achieved result] for [client type]"
- "I am going to show you something most [professionals] would never share"
- "This is what [number] per month in ad spend actually looks like"
- "Behind the scenes of a [impressive result] campaign"
- "Let me walk you through exactly what we did"

## Comparison Hooks

- "[Option A] vs [Option B] -- I tested both. Clear winner."
- "Why I stopped [old approach] and switched to [new approach]"
- "Everyone says [A]. But [B] gets better results. Here is the data."
- "I ran the same [thing] two ways. The results were not even close."

## Controversy Hooks

- "[Common belief] is a lie. Here is the truth."
- "Unpopular opinion: [bold contrarian take]"
- "I am going to get hate for this but [honest statement]"
- "[Respected authority] is wrong about [topic]. Here is why."
- "The [industry] does not want you to know this"

## Hook Rules

1. The hook must land in the first 3 seconds of video or the first line of copy
2. Specificity beats vagueness -- use numbers, names, timeframes
3. The hook's job is to earn the next 5 seconds, not to sell
4. Test 3 hook variations per ad -- same body, different hooks
5. Winning hooks can be reused across different angles and formats
6. Track which hooks perform best in your Airtable swipe file -- let data drive decisions
